## Key Insights

1. **Dev notes as forward contracts work** — the pattern of explicitly warning the next story about known gotchas reduced bugs before they were written.
2. **Deferred debt compounds if not gated** — items 1 and 2 from Epic 1 were "must fix before Epic 2" and didn't land. The pattern needs a hard gate, not just intent.
3. **Complexity jumps at Epic 3** — first Supabase CRUD, first Server Actions, first authenticated app shell. Starting clean is non-negotiable.
